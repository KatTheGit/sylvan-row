A lioness and a beloved queen, despite her past. No matter
how powerful her time magic, she can't fix what she did.

PRIMARY - Wrath
  An AOE sword slash.

SECONDARY - Rectify
  Teleports a few seconds in the past, and gains
  a small amount of health.

DASH - Combat Leap
  Dashes forward, taking reduced damage.

PASSIVE - Dedication
  The higher her secondary charge, the faster
  her attack rate.
