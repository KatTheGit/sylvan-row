A hyena cop who was fired for her tendency
to solve problems with rocket launchers.

PRIMARY - The Subtle Art of a Rocket Launcher
  A three-hit combo, with each attack gaining
  in range.

SECONDARY - Rocket Jump
  Launches a rocket next to herself that damages
  opponents in an AOE and boosts her forward.

DASH - Hasty Dispatch
  Holding DASH lets her wallride to move quickly
  alongside walls.

PASSIVE - Save the City
  Heals nearby damaged walls.
