PRIMARY - Buck-Shot
  Mid-range attack with damage fall-off.
  Landing it empowers his DASH.

SECONDARY - Guardian Angel
  Holds up a shield. Taking damage costs
  secondary charge.
  If this ability is active, PASSIVE's speed
  is no longer applied.

DASH - Intervene
  A long dash. If empowered, heals allies
  and damages opponents in his path.

PASSIVE - Inspire
  Makes nearby allies faster.
  SECONDARY can only charge passively
  a few seconds after use.

A strong pious deer with the noble mission of
protecting those who can't defend themselves.
