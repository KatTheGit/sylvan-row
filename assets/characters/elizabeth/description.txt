A mischevious raccoon who uses gadgets,
gizmos and fate to his advantage.

PRIMARY - Cunning Knives
  Throws a knife that can bounce off walls, and
  that stays on the ground.
SECONDARY - Thingymajig
  Creates a walking turret that pinballs around
  while shooting any nearby opponents.
DASH - Recall
  Dashes and recalls all grounded knives. The
  knives damage and slows opponents in their
  path.
